You don't want to duplicate any content if that is not necessary. The more copies you make the more complex your maintenance will be. We found that this is especially a concern when you want to reuse questions from an assessment in an e-Learning course (or visa versa), when you just want to publish the questions from a course to memory training software or when you want to publish a course to a mobile device.
In order to solve this, easygenerator offers you the 'page link' and the 'content link'.
