Welcome to this introduction course of easygenerator. It is meant as an example so you know how your publication will look.

On the first page you will see the learning objectives from your course. The learner can click one. If he does he will see the first page of content with the question presented on the bottom. Progress is made when a question is answered correct. When answering a question the user will get feedback on his answer and can press next for the next page with content and the attached question. After the last content page the page with learning objectives will be shown again. After finishing all the objectives the learner can press finish. The result will be stored in the TinCan database.
